# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Property operations admins managing rentals, tenancy records, deductions, and reporting for a portfolio of residential properties.

## Product Purpose
Provide one operational workspace to capture property and tenancy data, monitor late collections and expiry risks, and prepare statement, P&L, and cash report outputs.

## Brand Personality
Calm, precise, practical.

## Anti-references
- Marketing-first landing patterns.
- Decorative dashboard card spam.
- Complex accounting visualizations before business rules are confirmed.

## Design Principles
- Keep data entry fast with focused pop-out panels.
- Prioritize scanning and operational status over presentation copy.
- Show implemented formulas where source logic is available and clearly mark remaining pending business rules.
- Keep all critical interactions keyboard-accessible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG AA contrast and focus visibility.
- Support keyboard navigation across nav, dialogs, tabs, tables, and form fields.
- Honor reduced motion preferences.
